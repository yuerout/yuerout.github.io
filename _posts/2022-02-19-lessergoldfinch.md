A week ago when I walked past the cherry trees (?) near Soga farm, I saw two cute lesser goldfinches jumping around. That was one of the most beautiful scenes I've seen -- two little creatures enjoying their lives in a rain of flower petals. I regreted not bringing my camera with me, but luckily they were still there the next week when I revisited them.
