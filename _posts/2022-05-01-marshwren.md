I hate and love marsh wrens at the same time. They're so lovely and adorable, but it's so hard to spot them. They made really loud sounds but somehow it was almost impossible to see where they were standing. When I was at Golden Gate Park, Merlin sound identification showed me that there were marsh wrens, but after spending at least 30 minutes walking around the spot I gave up.
