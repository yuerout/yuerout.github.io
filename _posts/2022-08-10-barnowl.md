I was wandering around the street at 10 PM, then I heard this sharp sound from the campanile of a university campus, so I walked there to see who's making that sound. When I looked up, I saw this barn owl standing on the patio, with it shadow on the wall. I ran back to my Airbnb to grab my camera, and luckily when I got back it's still there (it actually stayed there for quite a long time -- not sure why).
