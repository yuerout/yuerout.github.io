Went on a pelagic trip with [Legacy Whale Watch](https://www.sdwhale.com/)!!
I've always known that albatrosses are big and they have an incredible wingspan which made their incredible trips across the globe possible. The wandering albatross at [Museum of Vertebrate Zoology](https://mvz.berkeley.edu/) was huge compared with other specimens. However, seeing an actual albatross in the middle of the ocean still surprised me. It was such a massive bird.
