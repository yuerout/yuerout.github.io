Went on a pelagic trip with [Legacy Whale Watch](https://www.sdwhale.com/)!!
Kinda surprised that I was able to capture a clear shot of this Cook's Petrel, because it was moving around very quickly. I learned that they flew all the way from Australia/New Zealand to here (San Diego). Birds always surprise me with their long-distance migrations!
