Woke up at 5 AM on Sunday morning for a pelagic trip with [Alvaro's Adventures](https://www.alvarosadventures.com/). DON'T ASK ME WHY APERTURE WAS F32 I DON'T KNOW WHY I DID THAT I'M CRYING. 

Also in my first pelagic trip I thought storm petrel was 暴风鹱 because storm corresponds to 暴风 and 鹱 describes Procellariiforms, which is the order that storm petrels are in. Turns out 暴风鹱 was northern fulmar, which is also very cooooool!
